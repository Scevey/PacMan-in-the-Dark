You can move through the menus by using left click on the mouse

In Game:
Move around the map using W,A,S,D to collect the pellets
You have a limited view of the map so be careful
Avoid the ghosts at all costs, they cannot be killed but will kill you if they hit you.
Big Pellets will allow you to see the full map for a short time.
Your hunger bar will depelete if you go too long without getting pellets if it empties
you will die.
Hit escape if you wish to pause the game
Try and survive as long as you can!
